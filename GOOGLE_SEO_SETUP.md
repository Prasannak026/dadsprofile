## üìã Step-by-Step: Get on Google (5-10 minutes)

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console

1. Go to **[Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/)**
2. Click **"Start Now"** and sign in with Google account
3. Choose **"URL prefix"** property type
4. Enter: `https://prasannak026.github.io/dadsprofile/`
5. Click **"Continue"**

### Step 2: Verify Ownership

Google will ask you to verify you own this website. **Easiest Method:**

1. Choose **"HTML tag"** verification method
2. Copy the meta tag they provide (looks like: `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="ABC123...">`)
3. **Send me this tag** or add it to `index.html` in the `<head>` section (right after the charset line)
4. Push changes to GitHub
5. Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment
6. Click **"Verify"** in Google Search Console

### Step 3: Submit Sitemap

Once verified:
1. In Google Search Console sidebar, click **"Sitemaps"**
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**
4. Google will start crawling your site within 24-48 hours

### Step 4: Request Indexing (Optional but Faster)

For immediate indexing:
1. In Google Search Console, go to **"URL Inspection"** (top bar)
2. Paste: `https://prasannak026.github.io/dadsprofile/`
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
4. Google will index your homepage within hours instead of days
